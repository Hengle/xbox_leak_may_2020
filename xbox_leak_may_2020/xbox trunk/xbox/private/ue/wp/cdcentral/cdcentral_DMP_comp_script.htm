DirectMusic Producer Components

Script

Scripts are collections of little programs (routines) that implement audio in your game. Audio Scripts can tie into game events and survey game state variables (e.g., player health) to make appropriate musical or sound decisions. If the sound designer/composer wants to control playback, he can create a script file that manages the way the audio content is played. Scripts allow the sound designer/composer to work with a more simplified language, and provide them with much more control in actually implementing music and sound effects in interactive situations.
