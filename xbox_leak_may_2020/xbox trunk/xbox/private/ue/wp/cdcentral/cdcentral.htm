Content & Design Central

Welcome to the Content & Design Central section of the Xbox™ video game system Web site. The content on this site is generated by the Xbox Content and Design Team and is specifically aimed at producers and content creators (artists, designers, composers, and sound designers). This site addresses the issue of how content creators can take advantage of the Xbox console's powerful features to create next-generation experiences. We will continually be posting new content to this site. If there is a topic that you would like to see covered, or if you want to submit feedback on existing content, please e-mail content@xbox.com.

    Note   For content based on formal and informal studies performed by the Xbox Game Evaluation Team and specifically aimed at producers and designers, see the Xbox Game Evaluation section of the site.

    Note   In reaction to requests from developers, an art newsgroup has been created on Xbox Central. This forum can be used to ask questions, discuss art and graphics issues, and share insights with other artists who are working on Xbox games. As with all newsgroups, the comments and information exchanged is not screened for accuracy by Microsoft. I will be monitoring the newsgroup and will chime in if I have anything to offer. To join the art newsgroup, go to the newsgroups section from the main menu and follow the instructions to join.

    - Dave McCoy, Graphics Techniques Consultant, Xbox Advanced Technology Group
