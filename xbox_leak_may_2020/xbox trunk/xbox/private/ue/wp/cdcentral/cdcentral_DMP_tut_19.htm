DirectMusic Producer 7.0 Tutorial

Lesson 19

The mixing grid is on the right side of the Band Editor. Up and down represents volume; right and left represents stereo pan. Move the icons with PChannel numbers in the grid to establish a ballpark mix (to be tweaked later).
