Best Practices Guide – Audio Designer's Corner

The Best Practices Guide for Xbox audio design is designed to help familiarize you with the process of developing and integrating audio content for Xbox. Because of the broad scope of this discussion, the guide is divided into several chapters. The intent is that earlier chapters will help you get a good jump on developing content on your PC for Xbox, while later chapters (and separate white papers) will drill down into more specific content authoring concepts and concerns.
